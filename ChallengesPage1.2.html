Transitioning

Developing and deploying applications on the cloud is a relatively simple process however transitioning existing applications can become a costly and time-consuming process.

Outages

Outages Outages are a big risk of using cloud-based systems. A loss of power or connectivity issues to data centres can leave companies without access to crucial information and software, severely impacting their business.
